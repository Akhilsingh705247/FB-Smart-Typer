Smart Typer Options

Smart Typer gives quick autocomplete suggestions to your Facebook chatbox.

It allows user to create a custom dictionary for words not present in the dictionary.
Your custom dictionary is also used for suggestions.

Also, it is synced with your extension. So you can access it on your other devices having this extension.However, this space is not infinite.(Look Here: Sync Storage)

You can add words to this dictionary here as well as from Facebook chat itself.
Also, here you can remove certain words and even completely reset your dictionary.

  • Add a Word(here) : Click the input field, input a word and click "Save" button.(Don't forget to save else word will not be added)
    Words must have:
    • Atleast length of 2 letters
    • No spaces
  • Remove a Word : Click "x"(remove) buttom besides the word.
  • Reset Custom Dictionary : Click "Reset" button to clear your custom dictionary/remove all words.
